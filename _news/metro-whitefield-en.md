# Major Infrastructure Milestone for Bengaluru

The Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL) today commenced construction on the highly anticipated Whitefield metro extension. The groundbreaking ceremony was attended by Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai, Bengaluru Development Minister K. J. George, and other dignitaries.

## Project Details

- **Route**: Byappanahalli to Whitefield
- **Length**: 15.5 kilometers
- **Stations**: 12 new stations
- **Expected Completion**: 2025
- **Estimated Cost**: â‚¹4,000 crore

### Benefits for Commuters

This extension is expected to significantly reduce traffic congestion on the Old Madras Road and provide a reliable public transportation option for the IT corridor in Whitefield. 

> "This project will transform the daily commute for thousands of tech professionals and residents in the eastern corridor of Bengaluru," said the Chief Minister during the ceremony.

The construction will be carried out in phases with minimal disruption to existing traffic. Regular updates will be provided to the public through the BMRCL website and local news channels.
